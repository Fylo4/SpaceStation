Goals

Draft Written 4/24/2025 Edited 4/24/2025

I think it is healthy to have an overarching goal to one's life. What is the ultimate goal you're pursuing?

There are a few goals I believe are ultimately unfulfilling in themselves. They may be used as instrumental goals, but not as main goals.

  • Wealth accumulation
  • Power or influence
  • Hedonism

There are several goals I think are valuable as overarching goals:

  • Furthering knowledge and science
  • Eudaimonia
  • Artistic expression
  • Flourishing social connections
  • Improving the lives of others
  • Spiritual attainment
